Got questions?

What is VendorIQ?
VendorIQ is an AI bookkeeper that lives inside your WhatsApp. It records your sales, tracks customer debts, and sends you daily revenue reports. No app, no dashboard — just text.
Does it work on any phone?
Yes! Since it works over WhatsApp, it works on any phone — Android, iPhone, or even a basic feature phone. No smartphone required.
How much does it cost?
VendorIQ is currently in free beta. You can use all features at no cost. We'll announce pricing when we exit beta.
How do I delete a record?
Just text "delete my last sale" or "delete the 45k debt for Emeka" and VendorIQ will remove it. You can also say "cancel that" right after recording something.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.
